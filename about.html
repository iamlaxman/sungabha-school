About Our School

Established in 1994 (2050 BS), Sungabha Awasiya Ma Vi has been a center of academic excellence in Urlabari-5, Morang.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for their success in a rapidly changing world.

Academic Excellence

Providing high-quality education through innovative teaching methods and comprehensive curriculum.

Character Development

Fostering strong moral values, leadership skills, and social responsibility in our students.

Community Engagement

Building strong partnerships with parents and the community to enhance learning opportunities.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that nurtures future leaders and innovators who contribute positively to society while upholding the highest standards of academic excellence and moral values.

Innovation

Embracing new technologies and teaching methods to enhance learning experiences.

Leadership

Developing future leaders who can make positive changes in society.

Global Perspective

Preparing students for success in an interconnected world.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our approach to education and shape our school culture.

Excellence in Education
Integrity and Ethics
Innovation and Creativity
Respect and Diversity

Our History

Establishment (1994)

Founded with a vision to provide quality education to the community.

Growth & Development

Expanded facilities and programs to meet growing educational needs.

Present Day

A leading educational institution committed to excellence and innovation.

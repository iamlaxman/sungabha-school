Admission Process

Step 1: Submit Application

Complete and submit the online application form along with required documents.

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous Academic Records
  • Passport Size Photographs

Step 2: Entrance Test

Qualified candidates will be invited for an entrance test covering core subjects.

  • Mathematics
  • English
  • Science

Step 3: Interview

Selected students and parents will be called for an interview with school administration.

Our Academic Programs

Early Education Program

Our early education program focuses on developing fundamental skills and fostering a love for learning in young children. From PlayGroup to Primary level, we provide:

  • Age-appropriate curriculum
  • Interactive learning methods
  • Skilled and caring teachers
  • Safe and nurturing environment

Secondary Education (SEE)

Our secondary education program prepares students for the Secondary Education Examination (SEE) with:

  • Comprehensive curriculum coverage
  • Experienced teaching faculty
  • Regular assessments and feedback
  • Extra support classes

+2 Science Program

Our Science stream offers comprehensive preparation for future careers in science and technology:

  • Well-equipped science laboratories
  • Experienced subject specialists
  • Practical-based learning
  • Career counseling support

+2 Management Program

Our Management stream prepares students for successful careers in business and administration:

  • Business-oriented curriculum
  • Professional development workshops
  • Industry exposure
  • Career guidance
